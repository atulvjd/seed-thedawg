# Gait Planning

## Overview

Gait planning refers to the process of generating coordinated leg movement patterns for quadruped locomotion.

The purpose of gait planning is to:
- Maintain balance
- Generate movement
- Synchronize legs
- Control walking patterns

---

# Initial Concepts

Current gait research includes:
- Leg synchronization
- Alternating movement patterns
- Stability analysis
- Weight shifting
- Movement sequencing

---

# Planned Gait Types

Potential gait implementations:
- Crawl gait
- Trot gait
- Pace gait
- Bound gait

---

# Stability Considerations

Important factors:
- Center of gravity
- Foot placement
- Timing synchronization
- Weight distribution
- Movement smoothness

---

# Future Development

Future gait systems may include:
- Dynamic balancing
- Terrain adaptation
- Sensor feedback integration
- AI-assisted locomotion
- Real-time gait correction
